My photo gear

The wonderful Instax Mini Evo

The instax Mini Evo is for sure a quirky thing, being both a camera and an instant printer. Featuring a hybrid system that bridges the gap between analog and digital photography, this camera lets you take pictures, edit them, and only print the ones you choose. There is also an app that let's you send images directly to the camera to print. Every photo in the Instax gallery was printed and then digitally scanned from a physical copy using a printer.

Retro Revolution: The Samsung Digimax

The Samsung Digimax A6 camera is from a line of wonderful digital cameras from the early 2000s. Before we had our modern phone cameras with their incredible color accuracy, there were the digicams. Using CCD sensors for capturing images, although still digital, captured images give off more of an 'analog' feel. Soft blurs and yellow-greenish hues characterise the images these cameras produce. I enjoy shooting this camera for its characteristics. In a world full of perfectly crafted social media photos, shooting with this camera provides a breath of fresh air.
