The wonderful Instax Mini Evo

The instax Mini Evo is for sure a quirky thing, being both a camera and an instant printer. Featuring a hybrid system that bridges the gap between analog and digital photography, this camera lets you take pictures, then mix and match, edit them, and only print your favorite ones. There is also an app that let's you send images directly to the camera to print. Featuring a cold shoe and a tripod mount, this litte camera can be equipped for any kind of challenge.

Retro Revolution: The Samsung Digimax

The Samsung Digimax line is a series of wonderful digital cameras from yesteryear. Before we had our modern phone cameras with their incredible color accuracy, there were the digicams. Using CCD sensors for capturing images, although still digital, captured images give off more of an 'analog' feel. Soft blurs and yellow-greenish hues characterise the images these cameras produce. Lately the old digicams of yesteryear became sought after again, as more and more people appreciate the characterful imperfections they provide in a world of perfectly crafted social media posts.
