The wonderful Instax Mini Evo

The instax Mini Evo is a quirky little camera, featuring a hybrid system which bridges the gap between analog and digital photography. Featuring a cold shoe and a tripod mount, this litte camera can be equipped for any kind of challenge. There is also an app which let's you send images directly to the image to print.

Retro Revolution: The Samsung Digimax

The Samsung Digimax line was a series of wonderful digital cameras, using the now defunct and sought-after CCD sensors of yesteryear. Before we had our modern phone cameras with incredible color accuracy, there were the digicams. Using CCD sensors for capturing images, although still digital, images give off a more 'analog' feel. Soft blurs and yellow-greenish hues characterise the images these cameras produce. Right now having a resurgence, the old digicams of yesteryear became sought after again, as more and more people appreciate the characterful imperfections they provide, in a world of perfectly crafted and edited social media posts. Paired with the nostalgia factor, the old digicams make every shoot seem like a dreamish sequence because of their imperfections.
