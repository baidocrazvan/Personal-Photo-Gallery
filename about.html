Hello. My name is Razvan and I'll be short. We all have hobbies, I personally have a few. One of the things I enjoy the most is using interesting and/or quirky cameras, photo or video, old or new. I get bored by the "perfect"-ly bland photos I see every day on social media. I sometimes go out with one of my cameras, and if I see something mildy interesting I'll just take a picture. I created this website to have a place where I can upload my collection of photos and share with my friends. Every photo on this website is taken by me. Every photo in the Instax gallery is physically printed and then scanned. My main goal for this website was for it to be simple (just CSS and JS), and to be able to add photos in the future HTML without needing to adjust the code or styling.
